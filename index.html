CGPA to Percentage — University Wise

VTU CGPA to Percentage Calculator

Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU) uses the formula: Percentage = CGPA × 10. So 8 CGPA = 80%, 7.5 CGPA = 75%, 9 CGPA = 90%. VTU is one of the largest technical universities in India with over 200 affiliated colleges.

Mumbai University CGPA to Percentage

Mumbai University uses the formula: Percentage = (CGPA – 0.5) × 10. So 8 CGPA = 75%, 7.5 CGPA = 70%, 9 CGPA = 85%. This formula applies to all faculties under University of Mumbai including engineering, commerce and arts.

Anna University CGPA to Percentage

Anna University uses the formula: Percentage = (CGPA – 0.75) × 10. So 8 CGPA = 72.5%, 7.5 CGPA = 67.5%, 9 CGPA = 82.5%. Anna University is the largest technical university in Tamil Nadu with over 550 affiliated colleges.

AKTU / UPTU CGPA to Percentage

Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU), formerly UPTU, uses: Percentage = (CGPA – 0.5) × 10. So 8 CGPA = 75%, 7 CGPA = 65%, 9 CGPA = 85%. AKTU is the largest technical university in UP with over 700 affiliated colleges.

KTU CGPA to Percentage

APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU) Kerala uses: Percentage = (CGPA – 0.5) × 10 for most programs. So 8 CGPA = 75%, 7.5 CGPA = 70%. Always verify with your KTU marksheet as the formula may vary by batch.

JNTU CGPA to Percentage

Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University (JNTU) uses: Percentage = CGPA × 10. So 8 CGPA = 80%, 7 CGPA = 70%, 9.5 CGPA = 95%. This applies to JNTU Hyderabad, JNTU Kakinada and JNTU Anantapur.

CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average) is used in India on a 10-point scale. GPA (Grade Point Average) is commonly used in the US on a 4-point scale. Our calculator supports both — select "4 point (GPA)" in the scale dropdown if your university uses the 4-point system.
In India, 60% and above is generally considered First Class, 75% and above is First Class with Distinction, 50–59% is Second Class, and 40–49% is Pass Class. This varies slightly by university.
To convert percentage to CGPA using UGC formula: CGPA = Percentage ÷ 9.5. For example, 76% ÷ 9.5 = 8 CGPA. For VTU: CGPA = Percentage ÷ 10. For Mumbai University: CGPA = (Percentage ÷ 10) + 0.5.
